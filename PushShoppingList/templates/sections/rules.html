Best Product Ranking

  • Ingredient match Products score higher when their names match more ingredient terms.
  • Food restrictions Avoid rules block a product. Required rules boost products that satisfy required terms.
  • Price Visible prices are preferred. Missing prices lower confidence and are saved with a skip reason.
  • Direct product URL Direct product pages are preferred over store search-result pages.
  • Distance Closer nearest-store locations improve ranking when distance is available.

Saved Product Choices

  • Best product The top viable candidate is saved as the selected product for the ingredient.
  • Alternatives All viable candidates are preserved so the Alternatives button can switch the selected product.
  • Manual override Manual alternative selections persist across refreshes and sync through the backend JSON state.
  • Fallback search pages Search-result fallback pages are kept as references, but they are not selectable as best products.
  • No valid product When no viable candidate is found, skip reasons are saved and shown instead of a product pick.

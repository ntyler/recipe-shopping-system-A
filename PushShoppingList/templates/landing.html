How it works

From meal idea to store-ready list.

The product acts as the missing layer between recipe inspiration, household inventory, and grocery checkout.

  1. Add recipesPaste links, upload a photo, or enter items manually.
  2. AI organizes ingredientsItems are cleaned, grouped, and tied back to each recipe.
  3. Check pantry, spices, and receiptsSee which items may already be available at home.
  4. Choose storesEnable the stores that matter for your grocery trip.
  5. Review and shopOpen store links, compare matches, and build the final trip.
